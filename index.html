Who We Are

Friends of Fieldway is committed to strengthening the local community by providing educational support, social activities, and wellbeing programmes for residents of all ages.

We believe that everyone deserves access to resources, skills development, and meaningful relationships to thrive.

Our Mission

To create a safe, supportive, and enriching space for individuals and families to connect and grow.

Our Vision

Transforming our building into a vibrant centre for education, mentorship, and creative expression.

Our Community Impact

By effectively utilizing this space, we aim to achieve the following:

🎓 Better Academics

Support students in achieving higher educational outcomes by overcoming specific academic challenges.

🛡️ Safe Spaces

Provide positive, structured activities as constructive alternatives for our young people.

🫂 Reduced Isolation

Foster connections among adults and elderly residents through dedicated support networks.

🎨 Cultural Expression

Encourage creativity and artistic development through workshops, reading, and poetry.

🔗 Stronger Bonds

Strengthen community relationships to create a unified cornerstone for community life.
