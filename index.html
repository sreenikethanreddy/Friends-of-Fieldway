Who We Are

Friends of Fieldway is committed to strengthening the local community by providing educational support, social activities, and wellbeing programmes for residents of all ages.

Our Mission: To create a safe, supportive, and enriching space where individuals and families can access resources, develop skills, and build meaningful relationships.

Our Vision: Transforming our building into a vibrant centre for education, mentorship, creative expression, and community support.

Our Core Programs

📚

Academic Support

Structured homework clubs and one-on-one tutoring to help primary and secondary students build confidence and improve educational outcomes.

🎯

Youth Engagement

Safe, structured environments featuring creative workshops, sports, and leadership opportunities to foster positive development.

🤝

Community Support

Dedicated women's and men's groups focused on wellbeing, skills development, and building supportive friendships to reduce isolation.

Our Community Impact

By effectively utilizing this space, we aim to achieve the following:

  • Support students in achieving better academic outcomes.
  • Provide safe and positive activities for young people.
  • Reduce social isolation among adults and elderly residents.
  • Encourage creativity and cultural expression.
  • Strengthen community relationships and collaboration.
